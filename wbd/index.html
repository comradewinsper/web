Our Story

Founded in 2013, WBD started as a simple tool to schedule tweets. We quickly realized that creators, small businesses, and agencies all faced the same problem: social media was too complex and time-consuming.

Today, we're a team of 73 people spread across 15 countries, serving over 190,000 active users. We've been profitable from day one and have never taken any venture capital, allowing us to focus 100% on our customers and our team.

Transparent Salaries

We believe in fair and open pay. We use a simple formula to calculate salaries and share them publicly to ensure fairness and accountability.

Our Salary Formula

Salary = Role Base + (Role Base × Cost of Living) + (Role Base × Experience Factor)

We publish all our salaries in a public spreadsheet, so there's no mystery and no negotiation. Everyone with the same role and experience level is paid the same, adjusted for their local cost of living.
